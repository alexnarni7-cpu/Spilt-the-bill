# Split the Bill

Scan a receipt, assign each item to the person who ordered it, and split tax & tip
**proportionally** — so everyone pays their fair share. Installable as a phone app,
works offline, and nothing you enter is ever saved.

**Live app:** https://split-the-bill-07.netlify.app/
